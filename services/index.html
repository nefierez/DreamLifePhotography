One Session at the Time

At DreamLifePhotography, we believe that each client is unique and deserves a personalized photography experience. One session at a time, we provide high-quality and memorable photographs that showcase your personality and style. Our ability to turn photographs into beautiful works of art is something we take great pride in, and we ensure that our clients can cherish them for a lifetime. From weddings to family portraits, we offer a range of photography services to suit your needs.

Weddings

Being allowed to be the one who perpetuates the big day of a bridal couple is a great honor. And a huge responsibility. There is a lot going on on this day, and I want to be there documenting small and big moments, while being as visible as possible. My experience provides the security needed on one of their greatest days in life. Then you can only focus on enjoying your day to the fullest with family and friends!

Couples

Couples shoots are the bomb! Couples are one of my favorite things to shoot, as they are all about having fun, not having to worry about the white dress getting dirty or anything like that! A shoot like this is perfect if you are engaged and want photos to use for save the date-cards, invitation or other printed goods.

Families

Investing in family photography is just that, an investment. Gathering the whole family and getting pictures and perpetuating the interaction between you is something that is irreplaceable and not something you regret having done. Family photography is a fun and unpretentious moment that we also try to fill with a little play and laughter, especially for the children.

Birthdays

We offer the finest photography sessions in birthday party and capture gorgeous photos of the birthday boy/girl along with the guests present. We conduct a professional photo shoot of all the special moments in a birthday party like cake cutting, musical program, dance, guests, and other special programs organized in the party. Our motive is to capture all the happiest moments of birthday function in our camera and showcase them in a mesmerizing album for future memories.

Graduations

The day you graduate is a momentous occasion, and for most people a once-in-a-lifetime event. Make sure your memories of the special day don't get lost in your phone's camera roll. You can get our services to have your graduation photos that you can proudly hang on your wall.

Newborns

The first few weeks at home with a newborn baby are a very special time in life. I am delighted to be able to offer newborn photography, done in peace and quiet in a safe environment in their own home, a so-called 'newborn lifestyle' photography. Here are less posed pictures, and more focus on the baby with parents / family in the home. This photography should not be stressful, but a great experience for creating memories that you can look back on later.
