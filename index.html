Snapping an Event into Beautiful Memories...

Dream Life Photography offers high-quality photography services for various events, including weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and family portraits. Dream Life Photography takes pride in their ability to turn photographs into beautiful works of art that clients can cherish for a lifetime.

For that reason, we have created a meticulous process that ensures that our clients receive the best possible photography service. From the initial consultation to the final delivery of the edited photographs, we pay close attention to every detail to ensure that our clients are satisfied with the end product.

1 ~ ABOUT THE SESSION

We offer personalized photography packages, including photography and videography services, tailored to your specific needs. Our goal is to create a unique photography plan that exceeds your expectations. We also coordinate with web or graphic designers to ensure that the images and motion meet their needs. Let's work together to create beautiful memories.

2 ~ PLANNING

We go beyond just taking photographs and believe that a successful photoshoot involves careful planning and preparation. When you book a session with us, we include the following services:

  • An introductory consultation where we get to know you and your needs
  • Understanding your expectations
  • Photoshoot production, which includes location scouting, color scheme selection, wardrobe coordination, prop selection, hair and makeup, and an inspiration board.

3 ~ BOOKING

Dream Life Photography makes booking your session easy. Simply choose a convenient date and time and send a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Their team will finalize all details including location, wardrobe, and more to ensure a seamless and enjoyable photoshoot experience.

4 ~ DELIVERY

You'll receive an online gallery of lightly edited images within two weeks of your session. We'll identify the number of final, fully-edited images you'll receive based on your chosen package. We'll provide you with a preview and implement your feedback before delivering high-quality, fully-edited images that exceed your expectations.
